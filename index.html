Integrated Solutions for Energy Assets

Nexcore Energy Co. Limited delivers integrated, end-to-end solutions across the oil and gas value chain. Our operations are structured into four core segments: technical field services, strategic procurement, project management, and maintenance. Together, these pillars enable us to support our clients with efficiency, safety, and operational excellence.

Technical Field Operations

Specialized on-site services that optimize production and maintain integrity.

  • Wellhead maintenance & intervention
  • Flow station & pipeline servicing
  • Pressure testing & hot tapping
  • Surface facility commissioning
  • HSE audits & safety implementation

Supply Chain & Logistics

Ensuring cost-effective acquisition of quality equipment from global vendors.

  • Drilling & production consumables
  • Valves, fittings & pressure control
  • Inventory & just-in-time delivery
  • Vendor qualification & inspection
  • Warehousing & freight forwarding

Project & Asset Oversight

Structured frameworks to de-risk projects and control lifecycle costs.

  • End-to-end project planning (EPC)
  • Contract & contractor management
  • Performance reporting & cost control
  • Turnaround maintenance coordination
  • Risk & regulatory advisory

Asset Integrity & Reliability

Comprehensive maintenance solutions to maximize uptime and lifecycle.

  • Preventive & predictive programs
  • Corrective & breakdown maintenance
  • Turnaround & shutdown execution
  • Rotating equipment & machinery
  • Instrumentation & facility maintenance
